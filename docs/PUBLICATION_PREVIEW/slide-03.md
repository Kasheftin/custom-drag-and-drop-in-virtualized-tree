# Slide 3 — The midpoint trick (and its catch)

**Rendered image:** [`slide-03.png`](../assets/carousel/slide-03.png)

**Purpose:** Introduce fractional / lexicographic ranks — the standard fix — and foreshadow its limit.

**On-slide text**

- Headline: **Put a rank *between* the neighbours. Write one row.**
- Body: LexoRank makes ranks that always have room in the middle — until the middle gets crowded.
- Caption under the visual: `0|a00000:`  →  `between`  →  `0|m00000:`

**Visual**

- Two rank pills with a new one generated at their midpoint (one write, highlighted green).
- A faint "…but keep inserting in the same spot…" ghost showing the gap shrinking.
